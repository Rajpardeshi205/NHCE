Green Oasis Blooms at NHCE: A Thriving Eco-Friendly Initiative

In a commendable move towards sustainability, NHCE College Campus has embarked on a green journey with the initiation of a tree plantation drive. Transforming the campus into a lush haven, students and faculty alike are actively participating in this eco-conscious endeavor, fostering a sense of environmental responsibility.


The campus, once dominated by concrete structures, now boasts vibrant greenery, as saplings of various native species find their home in strategic locations. This thoughtful initiative not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of the campus but also aligns with global efforts to combat climate change and promote biodiversity.


NHCE's commitment to tree plantation reflects a broader dedication to nurturing environmentally conscious leaders, fostering a greener tomorrow. As these saplings take root, they symbolize the college's dedication to creating a sustainable and eco-friendly educational environment.
